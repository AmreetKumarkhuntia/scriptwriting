## Primary (recommended)

April 2026 mein ek poore hafte ke liye duniya ke **top 6 most-used AI models sab China ke the.** Na OpenAI, na Anthropic, na Google.

Aur yeh koi fluke nahi tha. Aaj OpenRouter par use hone wale **~61% AI tokens Chinese models se aate hain** — woh bhi GPT-5.5 se **17x se 50x tak saste** models se.

Is video mein hum dekhenge: yeh shift actually hua kaise, iske peeche kaunse 8 labs hain, unhone apne hundreds-of-millions-dollar models **free / open-source** kyun kar diye, market ne itni tezi se switch kyun kiya — aur woh ek baat jo koi nahi bata raha: **jab poori duniya kuch hi foundation models par build karne lage, toh control kiske paas jaata hai?**

Yeh OpenAI vs China ki ladai nahi hai. Yeh global AI ke *infrastructure* ke shift ki kahani hai — aur zyadatar log bina samjhe usi ke upar build kar rahe hain.

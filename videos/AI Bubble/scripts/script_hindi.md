## THE CHINA PROBLEM (1:15–2:00)

Kyunki kuch aisa hua hai jo "baad mein" ko bahut kareeb le aaya hai.

China ne entry maar di.

DeepSeek — ek Chinese AI lab — abhi API access de raha hai **$0.14 per million tokens pe.**

Claude Opus ka output pricing? **$25 per million.**

Yeh hai **89 guna ka price gap.**

Aur bhai, DeepSeek koi toy nahi hai. Coding benchmarks pe yeh Western models ko seedha compete karta hai.

Google pehle hi jhuk gaya. June 2026 mein unhone apna AI subscription **40% sasta** kar diya.

OpenAI ke saame ab ek impossible choice hai. Chinese prices match karo — aur paisa aur tezi se jalao. Ya prices rakho — aur users ko jaate dekho.

Dono mein se koi bhi achha option nahi hai unke liye.

## FONTS

### Primary Title Font
**Bebas Neue**

Used for:
- Video titles
- Section headers
- Map labels
- Country names
- Emphasis text
- Thumbnail text

Examples:

CHINA'S AI TAKEOVER

THE SHIFT BEGINS

AMERICA MISSED THIS

---

### Secondary Body Font
**Montserrat**

Used for:
- Explanations
- Statistics
- Lower thirds
- Captions
- Callouts
- Timelines

Examples:

For one week, the six most-used AI models
in the world were all made in China.

The trend accelerated much faster
than most analysts expected.

---

### Font Hierarchy

#### H1 (Major Section Titles)
**Bebas Neue**

Example:

CHINA TAKES THE LEAD

#### H2 (Subsections)
**Bebas Neue**

Example:

THE OPEN-SOURCE ADVANTAGE

#### Body Text
**Montserrat Medium**

Example:

Chinese AI companies focused heavily on
open-weight models and rapid iteration.

#### Statistics
**Montserrat SemiBold**

Examples:

6 OF THE TOP 6 MODELS

72% MARKET SHARE

100M+ DOWNLOADS

---

### Maps

- Bebas Neue for countries
- Montserrat for labels and descriptions

## INTRO

Anthropic just dropped the most powerful AI model they've ever released to the public.

And here's the thing — two weeks ago, they said they *couldn't*.

Their top model, Mythos, had been locked away because it was too good at hacking. Not like "kinda dangerous." Like — it could find high-severity vulnerabilities in Windows, Chrome, and major browsers faster than most professional red teams. Governments were nervous. Anthropic was nervous.

Then on June 9th, 2026 — they released it anyway.

Well, sort of. Meet **Claude Fable 5**. Same Mythos intelligence. Guardrails on top. And the numbers it's putting up are actually insane.

Let me show you.

## STATS

Okay so the benchmark that matters most for coding is **SWE-Bench Pro** — this is the gold standard. Real GitHub issues. Real repos. Can the AI actually fix them end-to-end?

Fable 5: **80.3%**

GPT-5.5 — OpenAI's best public model: **58.6%**

Gemini 3.1 Pro: **54.2%**

That's not a close race. That's a 22-point gap over OpenAI and a 26-point gap over Google.

But that's the easy benchmark. Here's the scary one.

**FrontierCode Diamond** — this is the hard stuff. Novel codebases, complex multi-file tasks, the kind of work that takes a senior engineer a full day.

Fable 5: **29.3%**

Opus 4.8 — the previous best Claude: **13.4%**

GPT-5.5: **5.7%**

5.7 percent. Fable 5 is literally **5x better** than GPT-5.5 on the hardest coding tasks.

And it's not just code. On knowledge work — the GDPval benchmark that tests real business tasks across documents, analysis, reasoning — Fable 5 scores 1932. GPT-5.5 is at 1769. Gemini 3.1 Pro is at 1314.

Finance? **Harvey's Legal Agent Benchmark** — highest score ever. **Hex analytics** — first model to ever crack 90%.

Basically everywhere you look: Fable 5 is at the top.

Now — there's a catch. It costs double what Opus 4.8 costs. $10 per million input tokens, $50 per million output. That's expensive for heavy workloads.

But I wanted to actually *feel* the difference. Not just trust benchmarks.

## OUTRO

So — is Fable 5 worth it?

If you're a developer or building anything with AI — yes, probably. Especially for complex, long-running tasks. The gap on agentic coding is real and it's not small.

For everyday use? The price is steep. You might get 80% of the value from Opus 4.8 at half the cost.

But the fact that Anthropic shipped a Mythos-class model at all — after saying it was too dangerous — is the bigger story. Because Mythos is still out there. Still locked. Still being used by governments and defense contractors.

Fable 5 is just the version they decided we could handle.

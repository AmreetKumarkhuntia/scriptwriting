# AI Won't Take Your Job (The Real Story)

## Core Thesis
The "AI will destroy jobs" narrative is built on the Lump of Labor Fallacy, ignores 200 years of tech history, and is directly contradicted by 2023–2026 actual employment data.

## Working Title Options
1. "AI Will Not Take Your Job (Here's The Actual Data)"
2. "Everyone Said AI Would Kill 300 Million Jobs. They Were Wrong."
3. "The Biggest Lie About AI and Jobs"

## Premise
Every major technology wave — the spinning jenny, the steam engine, the internet — was supposed to end work as we know it. None of them did. AI is following the same pattern, and the data from the last 3 years proves it.

But there's a twist nobody talks about: the jobs AI can't replace tend to be blue-collar. And AI might actually restore the middle class that computers already hollowed out.

## Emotional Arc
Fear → Skepticism (based on history) → Data → Optimism (with nuance)

## Target Audience
Indian working-age adults, 20–35, anxious about their career in the AI era.

## Content pillars
1. **AI Wars** — OpenAI vs Anthropic vs Google vs Chinese labs; DeepSeek vs GPT.
2. **AI Economics** — inference costs, why AI companies burn billions, who actually profits.
3. **AI Infrastructure** — Nvidia's moat, data centers, agent infra, compute bottlenecks.
4. **AI Strategy** — why a lab changed direction; why open-source is winning.
5. **Future of Software** — will agents replace apps? will SaaS survive?

## Audience
- **Primary:** software/AI engineers, founders, PMs, technical students, AI enthusiasts.
- **Secondary:** investors, tech professionals, business analysts.

## Format (per video)
Strong hook → surprising statistic → what changed → why it changed → consequences → closing question.

## Thumbnail style
Large claim, few words, high contrast, one dominant visual (logos / maps / AI imagery).
e.g. "China Won AI" · "OpenAI Has A Problem" · "The End of SaaS?" · "DeepSeek Changed Everything".

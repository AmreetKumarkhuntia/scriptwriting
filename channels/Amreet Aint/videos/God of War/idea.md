# God of War (2018) — Highlight · idea.md

## Thesis
Turn a **complete ~19.5-hour blind playthrough** (5 live streams that got ~5–14 live views each) into
one **boss-first, full-journey supercut** for Amreet Aint — every boss fight + the iconic "goosebumps"
story beats, in story order. Acts map 1:1 to the five streams, so it splits into a 5-part weekly
series later with zero re-planning.

## Hook (cold open, ~60–75s)
The moment the channel owner loves: **Kratos reclaiming the Blades of Chaos** — Athena's ghost taunts
"You will always be a monster," and he answers **"I know. But I am your monster no longer."** Flaming
blades + the stream's loudest real reaction ("now we're getting goosebumps / this is what God of War
is"). Optional 2–3s flash-tease of the Baldur neck-snap first. Hard-cut to title.

## ~Beats (acts = streams)
- **Act 1 — The Journey Begins** (P1): Faye's pyre / "Be better" → First Troll → **the Stranger
  (Baldur) fistfight** → Brok → mountain reveal.
- **Act 2 — New Realms** (P2→P3): Freya intro → **World Serpent rises** → Ogre → Alfheim hop →
  Atreus's grief-vision in the Light.
- **Act 3 — Gods of Asgard** (P3): **Hraezlyr dragon** → Mimir beheading → **Freya reveal**.
- **Act 4 — The Blades Return** (P4): Fafnir → **Magni & Modi + Magni kill** → Athena + **Blades of
  Chaos** → Helheim bridgekeeper → **"I was born a god."**
- **Act 5 — The Finale** (P5): the ~40-min **Valkyrie** struggle → Atreus kills Modi → **Baldur
  returns** → flung across realms → **mistletoe arrow → Baldur final boss → neck snap** → Freya's vow
  → Jötunheim → **mural / "I'm a giant" → ashes → LOKI reveal.**

## Why it works as a continuous reel

The session is a self-contained spy mission, so the cuts already chain
cause→effect: **gadgets → who you're hunting → enter his city → see how brutal he
is → the auction gambit → take the city → find the body → the villain turns on
you → escape.** Two villain monologues (#5, #12) bookend the arc; the
Greenway-sale (#8) is the mid-point spike; the scorpion gag (#14) lands the
exit on a laugh. Streamer reactions are dropped in at the punchlines (#8, #13).

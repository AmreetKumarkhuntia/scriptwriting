The mission: hunt down Bathma — a pirate king who built a kingdom out of a ship graveyard — in the city of steel and sand. The twist: the only way out of his auction is to sell my own teammate.

This is **Day 3** of my 007 First Light playthrough, cut down to just the best bits: the gadgets, the boss beats, the auction gambit, and the banter.

In this one:
• Q-Branch hands me the toys — the laser strap and a flash mine ("not quite a death ray")
• The target: Bathma, the child-soldier-turned-pirate-king, and his city Al Lef
• A market brawl, the fight pit, and Bathma's chilling "city of steel and sand" speech

## Thesis
The fastest reliable views for an 855-sub Hindi channel **right now** = **Granny**. On the 7-day hourly

## Hook (cold open)
Open on the **single scariest jumpscare/near-death** of the session + the loudest genuine Hindi scream — no
intro, no "hi guys". Then a 1-line verbal hook: *"Akela, raat ko, is ghar me band — 5 din me nikalna hai…
chaliye."*

## ~10-minute beat structure
- `0:00–0:15` **Cold open** — scariest jumpscare + scream (pulled from later in the run).
- `0:15–0:40` **Hook** — "akela, raat ko, 5 din me bachna hai".
- `0:40–1:30` **Setup** — locked in the house, the goal (find items / escape), first creak/near-miss.
- `1:30–8:30` **Escape attempts** — 4–6 dense beats; each ends on a scare/near-catch, then teases the next
  ("ab ye darwaza khulna nahi chahiye tha…"). Cut ALL filler. Zoom-punch the facecam on each Granny appearance.
- `8:30–9:30` **Climax** — the escape (or the death) — the payoff the title promised.
- `9:30–10:00` **CTA + chain** — "agar dar laga to subscribe — agle video me [next horror game] 😱".
